My name is Shubhangi Ranjan. I live in India in the state of Madhya Pradesh. My culture has also shaped my perspective towards life in general. I am currently in the senior year of my high school. I aspire to be an emotional intelligent leader. I have a great interest in the field of Quantum. I have a deep yearning to know the nature of reality. My Book

An Introduction To All Things Quantum For Intellectuals.

This book is a culmination of my long standing thirst to understand my existence as well as nature. I took many roads to reach here from science to suffice the logical me to altering my belief systems with spiritual books to suffice the introspective and whimsical me. I donned these theories and finally came to a conclusion of who I am as a soul and a mind. This book is on the bizarre but profound Quantum Theory which scientists and researchers adapted to with a fresh, new perspective while also retaining the old one. I hope you enjoy it and get the answers that you are seeking.
